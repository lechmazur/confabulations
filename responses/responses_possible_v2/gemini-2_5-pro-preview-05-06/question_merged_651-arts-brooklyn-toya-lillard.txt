<answer>Andre Zachery</answer>
<other>The article identifies Andre Zachery as "the Brooklyn choreographer." His work "Against Gravity: Flying Afrikans + Other Legends" explores themes of flight, and he states, "'Across our diaspora, the necessity of flight has been part of our survival mechanism.'" The work addresses historical narratives through figures like Fred Hampton, Harold Washington, and Ben Wilson. He is performing in 651 Arts' new space, which is in Brooklyn, near where 651 Arts began as an "outgrowth of the Brooklyn Academy" in Fort Greene, in response to community cultural needs.</other>
